Privacy Policy

Thank you for visiting the Randomizer portfolio. Protecting your privacy is important, and this page explains how data is handled when you browse or interact with the site.

Information Collection

This portfolio does not collect personal information unless you voluntarily provide it through the contact form. No analytics, tracking, or cookies are used beyond basic browser behavior.

Contact Form

If you send a message through the contact form, the information you provide (email address and message) is only used to reply to your inquiry. It is not shared with third parties.

Data Security

The site uses secure HTTPS connections to transmit data. Any personal information submitted through the form is handled with care and only retained as needed to respond to your request.

Changes to this Policy

This privacy policy may be updated over time. If changes are made, the updated date will be reflected on this page.
