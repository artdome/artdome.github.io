Privacy Policy

Thank you for visiting the Randomizer portfolio. Protecting your privacy is important, and this page explains how information is handled when you view or interact with the site.

Information Collection

This portfolio does not collect any personal information unless you voluntarily share it through the contact form. There are no analytics, tracking cookies, or third-party data collectors used on this site.

Contact Form

When you send a message using the contact form, the email address and message content are used only to respond to your inquiry. This information is not shared with external parties.

Data Security

All interactions are delivered over HTTPS. Any personal information submitted via the contact form is treated with care and kept only as long as needed to reply.

Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated over time. If changes occur, the modified version will be reflected on this page.
